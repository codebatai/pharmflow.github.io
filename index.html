About Codebat Technology

Codebat Technology Inc. represents a paradigm shift in computational drug discovery, combining classical high-performance computing with quantum-ready algorithms to accelerate pharmaceutical research and development. Our flagship product, PharmFlow Drug Discovery Module, integrates molecular dynamics simulations, machine learning models, and intelligent agents to provide comprehensive drug discovery capabilities from target identification to lead optimization.


The Quantum-Ready Edition v1.0 establishes the foundation for next-generation drug discovery by implementing hybrid classical-quantum algorithms, preparing for the quantum advantage in molecular simulation while maintaining practical utility on current hardware. Our modular architecture ensures seamless integration with existing pharmaceutical workflows while providing extensibility for future quantum computing resources.
